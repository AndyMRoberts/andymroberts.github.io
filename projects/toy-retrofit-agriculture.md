# Retrofitting a child’s toy for AI agriculture research

This project explores adapting an inexpensive, widely available children’s toy platform into a testbed for agricultural robotics and edge AI. The goal is a safe, low-cost vehicle for experimenting with sensing, power budgets, and on-device inference in settings that loosely mimic field constraints—without committing full research hardware until ideas are proven.

Work in progress: mechanical and electrical retrofit, sensor mounting, and integration with the same software stack used in broader agri-robotics research.
